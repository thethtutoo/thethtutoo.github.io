Company in Brief

Shwe Than Lwin was established in 1992 as a trading company in the area of importing cars, trucks and buses from Japan. Also imported batteries, tires, cement, construction steel, andother industry-related products.Tied up with several local and overseas companies in various types of businesses. Based on the economic climate, and market policy changes, it expanded its business in other segments such as construction, agriculture, mining, services, entertainment, machinery & engineering, transportation, tourism, media, etc.

Shwe Than Lwin was founded in 1992 as a trading firm with a sole purpose of contributing the emergence of market economy in Myanmar by serving local consumers and o ering job opportunities to the people of Myanmar to perform their professions with integrity. Our Logo

Pyit Tine Htaung (Daruma) is one of the most popular talismans of good luck in the Myanmar tradition, the doll is made of paper mache with hollow and round body. It is weighted on the bottom so it always stands up, even when pushed. Its symbol is rather consistent with Shwe an Lwin’s spirit, as the company always stand up positively even if failing, and always strive for our objectives.
