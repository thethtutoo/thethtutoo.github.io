Agriculture

One of business which the Shwe Than Lwin group has ventured into at the early stage. Established in 1998, the company is involved in vast yet diversified plantations ranging from mango, pineapple, rambutan, guava, pomelo, avocado, betel nut, cashew, macadamia, coffee beans, rubber and palm trees as well as paddy that have all the productions supplying to both the domestic and foreign markets. In total, there are approximately 14688 acres of land located in several parts of Myanmar.
