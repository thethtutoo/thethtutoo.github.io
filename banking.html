Shwe Bank

Introduction

Directorate of Investment and Company Administration under Ministry of Planning and Economic Development of the Union Government registered SHWE BANK to incorporate as a limited company on 14 February 2014 and the Central Bank of Myanmar granted a banking license on 28 July 2014.

Organization

The Bank’s Board of Directors is the highest authority and made up of Directors led by the Chairman. The Board lays down policies of SHWE Bank. In accordance with the policies established by the Board, Vice Chairman leads the Heads of Department to execute the day-to-day operations of the Bank adhering to the Bank’s procedures.

Management

Top Management is the team that guides Heads of Department to execute within the framework of the procedures in accordance with the policies laid down by the Board. Heads of Department execute the operational plans as directed by the Top Management.
