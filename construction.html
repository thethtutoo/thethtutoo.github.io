Construction

Shwe Than Lwin Construction Company Limited is a construction branch of the Shwe Than Lwin Group. Started in 1996, the company is designed to build modern buildings and infrastructure with skilled workers and latest construction techniques. Over the years, the company has become one of the leading realty and housing development companies in Myanmar.

The Company is currently involved in the following projects : Garden Housing , Retail Shopping Malls, Commercial Projects, Educational Projects, Industrial Parks, Infrastructure and Utilty Services.

Objectives

  • To meet the standards and win customer satisfaction

  • To ensure worksite safety and motivate employees to work efficiently

  • To improve the company’s image and make healthy profit
