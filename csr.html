RELIGION

Shwe Than Lwin is a family-owned diversified business entity. Owners of the company are all devoted Buddhists and majority of the employess are Buddhists too. Daw Than Sein, the Patron of the Company as well as the BOD member, is well known for her religious attitude and unlimited generosity throughout Myanmar. She has orchestrated countless religious donations in the name of Shwe Than Lwin Co., Ltd. Among them, the most prominent religious donations are Prayer-hall at Kyaik Ka Lo Pagoda, DhammaRakkhita Religious Hall, and an approach rampy at Kyaik Kha Me Pagoda. For her noble religious attitude and strong generosity, she has been rendered the title of Aggamahasirisudhammasingi ( Gracious Lady of The Great ) by the State.

EDUCATION

Since BOD memebers of Shwe Than Lwin are highly aware that the fundamental secret to the nation’s development lies in Education, STL has always encouraged and supported the education in Myanmar. By doing so, Shwe Than Lwin has built school and donated stationery to do its part in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

SOCIAL SERVICE

Shwe Than Lwin has done a great deal of social services. Among them, the most outstanding one is the construction and commencement of Shwe Than Lwin Home for the Aged. All kinds of elderly people in need of care are accepted despited their race and religion. Another noble CSR work of Shwe Than Lwin is donation of Fire Engines to the Fire Department which tremendously strengthened the fire fighting capacity of fire fighters. This is a crucial factor as this donation helps a lot in saving the properties of Myanmar citizens from widespread fire.
