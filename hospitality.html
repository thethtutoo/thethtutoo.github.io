Sky Star Hotel ( Yangon ) ( 4 Stars )

The Sky star hotel blends the elegance with gracious Myanmar hospitality which makes it the perfect base for taking in the city’s exotic and distincitve culture. Opened in September 2013, the four-star Sky Star Hotel is located in 51/B/1, East Horse Race course Road, Tamwe Township, a place that is within close proximity to the Yangon International Airport.

Golden Guest Hotel ( Nay Pyi Daw )

The Golden Guest Hotel is the first hotel project the group has established in the new capital - Nay Pyi Daw. An extraordinary place for guests because of its unique combination of local architecture, expressive decoration and artistry. Currently, the hotel has a collection of 111 rooms with the selection of Executive, Junior, Deluxe suites and Standard rooms that cater to the diverse preferences of the guests.
