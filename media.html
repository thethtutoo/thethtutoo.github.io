SKYNET

Sky Net was founded in March 2010. It is a leading Myanmar TV station with over 2,000 employees. Sky Net has initialted a Direct to Home ( DTH ) broadcasting service since November 2010 and in February 2011, it launched Multi-Play Services ( MPS ) system. It currently transmits over 100 channels with the majority of which are international Pay-TV channels including all major football leagues.

Moreover , there are in house channels such as Myanmar International, Health, Education, Tine Yin Thar ( Ethic Minorities), Police, Parliament ( Hluttaw ), and Agriculture. One distinctive advantage of SKY NET is that its monthly fees are less than US $14. Suuch a very reasonable price actually reflects the Sky Net’s goodwill on Myanmar citizens as its motto is “Sky Net for the People”.

Sky Net uses satellite transmission and therefore signal coverage is always 100 percent all over the country. Due to its goodwill and strong commitments towards its customers, Sky Net has achieved tremendous growth rate within a short time.

SHWE FM RADIO

The Golden Wave Co., Ltd runs one of the largest radio broadcaster - Shwe FM in Myanmar. Commenced in August 2009, the radio station was established after witnessing the tragedy of cyclone Nargis that caused massive death tolls. For that reason, Shwe Than Lwin’s Top Management was very moved and strongly felt that establishing a FM radio station with denser signal coverage could effectively reduce the death toll in the similar disasters in the future.

Over the years, the company has embraced tremendous growth of subscribers with its own production of contents through a wide variety of programmes ranging from commercial free music, premier sports and live events, news and comedies to exclusive talk and entertainment.

In the near future, the company will share a 200-meter tower in Yangon that supports its coverage in Ayeyarwaddy region. Importantly, the company will be moving in a direction of becoming the No.1 radio station in the country by continuously providing high quality programmes to the audience.

MITV ( Myanmar International Television )

MITV is a broadcast TV brand intended for international and local English speaking consumers, launched on 31st March 2010 based in Yangon. It is a celebration of all aspects of Myanmar culture from the ancient to the brand new. A platform to represent the people of Myanmar, in english language, free to view and available 24 hours a day. An accurate, balanced, independent and up to date source of Myanmar information.

The soul of the MITV television channel is born from its local news, that gives a stage and a voice to local people and their stories. A chance for Myanmar to speak to the world and the world to connect with Myanmar.

Global Telephone

Global Telephone Co., Ltd is another subsidiary under the Shwe Than Lwin Group. Established in early 2009 with 40 employees, the company is mainly involved in GSM expansion, Fiber backbone construction, VSAT solutions, SIM card supplying, and mobile-related projects. With the support of the SKY NET Media in terms of the expertise and resource sharing within the related field, the company over the years has been growing rapidly. Today, it owns a mobile switching center, base station center, as well as a base transceiver station construction for GSM/WCDMA. In addition, the company is in partnership with large digital securigy company Gemalto, as well as the well-known global telecommunication equipment provider Alcatel-Lucent Shanghai Bell. Several remarkable projects have been carried out within the past four years:

  • Myanmar Post and Telecommunication for 30 million mobile phone project

  • Phayagyi-Dawei ( 245 miles ) fiber backbone infrastructure project

  • Thane-Myawaddy ( 130 miles ) fiber backbone infrastructure project

  • SIM card distribution and manufactures top-up cards for Myanmar Post and Telecommunication

  • Business Development with the SATCOM oversea company for VSAT and Microwave projects
