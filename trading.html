Heavy Vehicles & Spare Parts Trading

Shwe Than Lwin Trading Co., Ltd. was the initial company established among the group of companies. The company was mainly invoved in buying and selling well-known brands of both new and reconditioned cars, trucks, buses, heavy vehicles and heavy equipment including those used in agriculture, and spare parts, as its core business, where Japan and Thailand are the major trading partners.

In the past, the company acted as a sole distributer for several agricultural machinery brands from Siam Kubota ( Thailand ), as well as the “Tokyo” brand of batteries from Siam Furukuwa.

Cement Distributor

Another commodity imported by Shwe Than Lwin Trading Company in the past was cement. As Myanmar is a developing country, the demand for cement is high because of the construction of houses and commercial buildings, as well as country’s infrastructure such as bridges, roads and dams. In fact, the Siam Cement Group from Thailand has partnered with Shwe Than Lwin Trading Company for many years.

Fertilizer Distribution

The company also ventured into the distribution of fertilizers, namely urea and compound fertilizers. As agriculture is the primary sector of Myanmar’s economy and with 23 million acres of cultivated lands, fertilizer features as one of the key commodities for Myanmar.
